## Objetivo
Can you convert the number 42 (base 10) to binary (base 2)?

## Solución
```
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
┌──(jars㉿kali)-[~]
└─$ echo "obase=2; 42" | bc

101010
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
┌──(jars㉿kali)-[~]

```
